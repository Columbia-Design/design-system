Buttons

A button is a graphic used to direct users’ attention to a task or action by hyperlinking to relevant information. They are also called call-to-action buttons, or CTAs. They are pill-shaped and either compliment or contrast with the primary color of the site or app.

Usage

Use buttons to engage users to begin or complete a task.
